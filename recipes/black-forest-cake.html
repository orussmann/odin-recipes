Black Forest cake

Description

Vegan Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte effortlessly transforms the cherished Black Forest Cake into a plant-based masterpiece. Each layer of this exquisite dessert reveals a story of innovation and tradition harmoniously intertwined. The moist chocolate cake layers, delicately infused with cacao's richness, pay homage to the classic cake's indulgence while adhering to ethical principles.

Nestled amidst the layers, the vibrant cherry filling bursts forth with the sweetness of ripe cherries, creating a symphony of flavors that dance upon the palate. This nod to nature's bounty encapsulates the essence of the Black Forest's lush surroundings and adds a touch of vibrancy to every bite.

Enveloping the cake is a cloud of velvety coconut whipped cream, a testament to the marvels of plant-based alternatives. This ethereal layer not only lifts the senses with its airy texture but also celebrates the ingenuity of compassionate cooking. Adorned with vegan chocolate shavings, each slice becomes a canvas where tradition, innovation, and ethics converge in a masterpiece of flavor and values.

Ingredients

For the chocolate cake layers:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar

For the cherry filling:

  • 2 cups pitted cherries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For the coconut whipped cream:

  • 2 cans (13.5 oz each) full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated overnight
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For assembly and garnish:

  • Vegan chocolate shavings or curls
  • Additional cherries for decoration-

Steps

  1. Prepare the chocolate cake layers:
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    3. In a separate bowl, whisk almond milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and apple cider vinegar.
    4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
    5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    6. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. Make the cherry filling:
    1. In a saucepan, combine pitted cherries, sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice.
    2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens and the cherries soften. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. Prepare the coconut whipped cream:
    1. Open the refrigerated cans of coconut milk without shaking. Scoop out the solid coconut cream from the top of the cans.
    2. In a mixing bowl, whip the coconut cream using an electric mixer until smooth and fluffy.
    3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and continue whipping until well combined. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  7. Assemble the cake:
    1. Place one cooled chocolate cake layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of coconut whipped cream on top.
    2. Spoon the cooled cherry filling over the whipped cream layer.
    3. Place the second chocolate cake layer on top of the cherry filling.
    4. Frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining coconut whipped cream.
  9. Decorate and serve:
    1. Garnish the cake with vegan chocolate shavings or curls and additional cherries on top.
    2. Refrigerate the cake for at least a couple of hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld and the cake to set.
